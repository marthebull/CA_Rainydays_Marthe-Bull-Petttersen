ABOUT US

We are a progressive outdoor company based in Bergen, Norway. With a group consisting of scientists, artists, designers and adventurers, we are pushing the boundaries of how outdoor clothing is made. Together with our customers, we are recycling, renting, repairing and reusing our way to a new, sustainable outdoor industry.

Our passion for outdoor sports, new technologies and craftsmanship has driven us - and still does - to create progressive clothing to enable you to freely enjoy and challenge yourself in the great outdoors.
